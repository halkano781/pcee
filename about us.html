ABOUT US

Who are we

Portable car engineering environment (PCEE) limited is a virtual garage that uses the latest technology in the automotive industry to achieve all the automotive needs under one platform by monitoring all the vehicle data remotely in real time. We process the data and use it to provide predictive, prescriptive and reactive maintenance for all the vehicles regardless of model, make and year.

Our commitment

We are committed to ensuring that no vehicle breaks down by providing timely repairs to car owners anytime anywhere. We strive to make sure that car owners are prepared ahead of time about upcoming car expenses hence have them prepare financially to address them. We believe that time is money and car owners can save money by avoiding unnecessary car repair expenses as well as ensuring maximum profits that can be caused by time wasted during car downtime.
